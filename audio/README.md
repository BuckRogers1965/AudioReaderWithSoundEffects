# Audio Directory

Place all background music, ambient tracks, and sound effects used by your VML scripts inside this folder.

You can include:
- Background/looping music
- Environmental ambience
- Short SFX clips
- Character cues or stingers

Files may be in WAV, MP3, FLAC, or OGG format.

## Finding Audio

You are free to use any source you prefer. Here are a few reliable starting points for royalty-free or open licensed audio:

- https://freesound.org — large community library of sound effects  
- https://pixabay.com/music — royalty-free music and SFX  
- https://mixkit.co/free-stock-music — high-quality free music loops  
- https://incompetech.com/music/royalty-free — Kevin MacLeod’s extensive catalog  

Be sure to follow the license terms for any audio you download.

Place your chosen files directly in this directory or in subfolders as needed.
